# Purpose

The Project Glossary defines the common terminology used across Project Compass.

As the platform evolves, new business concepts, technical terms, and product terminology will be added here.

This document ensures that everyone involved in the project speaks the same language and interprets important concepts consistently.

---

# Business Terms

## GL-001 - Search My Vacation

The travel company for which Project Compass is being built.

---

## GL-002 - Project Compass

The internal development initiative responsible for building the complete digital ecosystem of Search My Vacation.

Project Compass includes the website, future customer platform, internal tools, documentation, architecture, and technology roadmap.

---

## GL-003 - Travel Relationship Platform

A platform designed to build long-term relationships with travellers rather than focusing only on travel transactions.

Every feature should strengthen customer trust, engagement, and loyalty.

---

## GL-004 - Travel Consultant

A Search My Vacation team member who helps customers plan, customize, and manage their travel experiences.

Technology supports the consultant rather than replacing them.

---

# Product Terms

## GL-005 - Travel Collection

A themed grouping of destinations based on customer interests or travel styles rather than geography.

Examples:

- Family Holidays
- Romantic Getaways
- Beach Escapes
- Spiritual Journeys
- Weekend Getaways

A destination may belong to multiple Travel Collections.

---

## GL-006 - Destination

A place that customers can explore through Search My Vacation.

Each destination contains information, itineraries, travel confidence, blogs, and other supporting content.

---

## GL-007 - Itinerary

A day-wise travel plan curated for a destination.

Itineraries are designed as guidance and can be customized based on customer requirements.

---

## GL-008 - Land Package

The portion of a travel package that excludes flights.

Typically includes:

- Accommodation
- Transfers
- Sightseeing
- Local assistance
- Activities (where applicable)

---

## GL-009 - Wishlist

A personalized list of destinations, itineraries, or travel ideas saved by a registered customer for future reference.

---

## GL-010 - Trip Scorecard

A standardized visual comparison that helps customers evaluate destinations.

Typical attributes include:

- Family Friendly
- Senior Friendly
- Adventure
- Relaxation
- Shopping
- Scenic Beauty
- Budget

---

## GL-011 - Travel Confidence

A visual indicator that helps customers understand how suitable a destination is for their travel requirements.

It is intended to reduce uncertainty and support better travel decisions.

---

# Customer Experience Terms

## GL-012 - Personalization

The ability to customize recommendations, itineraries, and travel experiences according to customer preferences.

---

## GL-013 - Trust

The confidence customers place in Search My Vacation to recommend destinations honestly and provide memorable travel experiences.

Trust is the foundation of every product decision.

---

## GL-014 - Community

The ecosystem of travellers, repeat customers, partners, and travel enthusiasts connected through Search My Vacation.

---

# Technology Terms

## GL-015 - CRM

Customer Relationship Management.

A system used to manage leads, enquiries, quotations, customers, follow-ups, repeat travellers, and long-term customer relationships.

CRM is considered a strategic capability for the future growth of Search My Vacation.

---

## GL-016 - API

Application Programming Interface.

A secure mechanism through which two software systems communicate with each other.

Future integrations may include:

- Payment Gateway
- CRM
- Airline Services
- Hotel Partners
- Vendor Systems
- AI Services

---

## GL-017 - AI Assistant

A future capability that will assist customers and internal teams by answering travel questions, recommending destinations, and improving operational efficiency.

Human expertise will always remain central to the customer experience.

---

# Project Terms

## GL-018 - North Star

The guiding principle for all product and technology decisions.

> Create lifelong travel relationships through trust, personalization, and unforgettable experiences.

---

## GL-019 - Four Pillars

The four foundational principles that guide every feature within Project Compass.

- Trust
- Personalization
- Community
- Technology

---

## GL-020 - Inspiration Vault

A repository for ideas, observations, customer feedback, competitor analysis, and future enhancements.

Ideas are captured without immediate prioritization to encourage innovation.

---

## GL-021 - Architecture Decision Record (ADR)

A document that records significant architectural decisions along with their rationale and consequences.

ADRs help future team members understand why specific technical decisions were made.

---

## GL-022 - Decision Log

A chronological record of important business and product decisions made throughout the project.

---

This glossary is expected to evolve continuously as Project Compass grows.
