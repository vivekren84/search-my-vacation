# Purpose

The homepage is the digital front door of SearchMyVacation.

Its purpose is not merely to showcase destinations, but to build trust, inspire travel, answer visitor questions, and encourage meaningful enquiries.

Every section has been intentionally designed to support the traveller's decision-making journey.

---

# Design Philosophy

SearchMyVacation follows the **Traveller Decision Journey** approach.

Instead of presenting information based on business priorities, the homepage is organized around the natural questions travellers ask while planning a holiday.

The objective is to create an intuitive experience that feels helpful rather than promotional.

---

# Primary Goals

The homepage should:

- Inspire travel
- Build trust
- Showcase expertise
- Simplify destination discovery
- Encourage enquiries
- Highlight curated experiences
- Reinforce the SearchMyVacation brand promise

# Traveller Decision Journey

The homepage guides visitors through six questions:

1. Where can I go?
2. What kind of holiday suits me?
3. Why should I trust SearchMyVacation?
4. How do they make travel easier?
5. Have other travellers enjoyed their experience?
6. How do I start planning my trip?

Every homepage section exists to answer one of these questions.

# UX Principles

The homepage should:

- Tell a story from top to bottom.
- Minimize decision fatigue.
- Use progressive disclosure.
- Balance inspiration with practical information.
- Encourage exploration before commitment.
- Maintain clear visual hierarchy.
- Prioritize accessibility and responsiveness.

---
