# Journey Invitations Design Language

**Version:** 1.0

**Status:** Approved Design Foundation

**Owner:** Team Satvi

---

# Purpose

Journey Invitations define how SearchMyVacation invites travellers to discover journeys through emotion rather than transactions.

This design language ensures every invitation feels personal, inspiring and unmistakably human.

Rather than asking travellers where they wish to go, Journey Invitations help them discover why they wish to travel.

---

# Design Philosophy

Every invitation should feel like the beginning of a conversation.

Never a sales pitch.

We inspire before we recommend.

We understand before we suggest.

We earn trust before asking for commitment.

---

# Core Principle

> We don't push travellers towards bookings.

> We inspire them to begin a journey.

---

# Design Principles

## 1. Invite, Never Sell

Journey Invitations should encourage exploration.

Avoid language that pressures travellers into making decisions.

Preferred language:

- Explore this journey
- Discover more
- Tell me more
- Inspire me

Avoid:

- Book Now
- Buy
- Select
- Choose
- Limited Offer

---

## 2. Emotion Before Destination

Always communicate the feeling before mentioning the place.

Travellers connect with emotions.

Destinations simply fulfil them.

---

## 3. Photography Before Graphics

Use authentic lifestyle photography featuring real people experiencing meaningful moments.

Avoid decorative illustrations where photography can better express emotion.

The traveller should imagine themselves within the story.

---

## 4. Curated, Not Catalogued

Journey Invitations should resemble pages from a premium travel journal rather than products displayed in a catalogue.

Layouts should feel thoughtfully curated rather than mechanically arranged.

---

## 5. Editorial Composition

Visual rhythm is intentional.

Not every invitation should share the same size or position.

A gentle editorial hierarchy creates curiosity and encourages exploration.

---

## 6. Hero Invitation

The primary traveller voice receives visual prominence.

For Release 1:

**Memory Makers**

This reflects current traveller behaviour while remaining welcoming to every traveller type.

---

## 7. Organic Rhythm

Allow breathing space.

Avoid rigid grids where they reduce warmth.

Cards should feel naturally arranged rather than mathematically aligned.

---

## 8. Continuous Journey

Background colours, gradients and spacing should flow seamlessly from one section into the next.

The page should feel like one uninterrupted journey.

Never isolated blocks.

---

## 9. Human Conversations

Every headline should sound like a thoughtful conversation.

Every supporting sentence should spark imagination.

Marketing language should never overpower authenticity.

---

## 10. Respect the Traveller's Pace

Not every visitor arrives ready to enquire.

Some come to dream.

Some come to compare.

Some come to imagine.

SearchMyVacation respects every stage of that journey.

Our responsibility is not to rush decisions.

Our responsibility is to earn trust.

---

## 11. Pull, Don't Push

SearchMyVacation does not pursue aggressive sales.

We believe genuine inspiration creates stronger relationships than persuasive selling.

When travellers feel understood, trust naturally follows.

When trust exists, enquiries happen naturally.

---

# Journey Invitation Structure

Every invitation contains four elements.

## Emotional Photography

A meaningful human moment.

---

## Emotional Headline

A headline that reflects a traveller's aspiration rather than describing a destination.

---

## Imagination Sentence

One carefully crafted sentence that helps the traveller picture themselves within the experience.

---

## Gentle Invitation

A subtle invitation to continue exploring.

Never a hard call-to-action.

---

# Visual Language

Journey Invitations should feel:

- Warm
- Premium
- Calm
- Personal
- Elegant
- Spacious
- Editorial
- Timeless

Never:

- Busy
- Loud
- Promotional
- Discount-driven
- Transactional
- Overcrowded

---

# Success Measure

The design is successful when travellers pause, imagine themselves within a journey and continue exploring naturally.

SearchMyVacation should feel less like a booking platform and more like a trusted companion helping travellers discover experiences that truly matter.

The traveller should never feel hurried. They should feel welcomed.
