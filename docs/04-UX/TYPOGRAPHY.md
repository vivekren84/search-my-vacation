# Purpose

The Typography System establishes the visual language for written communication across Search My Vacation.

Typography is more than choosing fonts—it defines how travellers read, understand and emotionally connect with content.

The objective is to create a reading experience that feels trustworthy, approachable and effortless across all devices.

Typography should support the brand rather than compete with it.

# Typography Philosophy

Typography should never distract from the message.

Visitors should remember the experience and the stories they read—not the font itself.

Good typography quietly builds confidence while making every interaction comfortable and enjoyable.

The ultimate objective is simple:

> Typography should disappear.

When typography is designed well, travellers focus on the journey rather than the interface.

---

# Typography Personality

The Search My Vacation typography should feel:

- Warm
- Professional
- Friendly
- Calm
- Modern
- Trustworthy
- Premium
- Approachable

It should never appear:

- Loud
- Aggressive
- Overly decorative
- Corporate
- Difficult to read

---

# Typography Selection Criteria

The official typography for Search My Vacation shall satisfy the following criteria.

## Readability

Typography must remain highly readable across:

- Mobile
- Tablet
- Desktop

Long-form reading should remain comfortable.

---

## Simplicity

Typography should complement content rather than dominate it.

Decorative fonts should be avoided.

---

## Performance

Typography should:

- Load quickly
- Support web optimisation
- Minimise layout shifts

---

## Accessibility

Typography shall:

- Maintain sufficient size and spacing.
- Support WCAG AA readability recommendations.
- Remain readable under different lighting conditions.
- Avoid excessively thin font weights.

---

## Brand Alignment

Typography should reinforce the Search My Vacation brand by appearing:

- Trustworthy
- Human
- Premium
- Friendly

---

## Scalability

The chosen font family should support:

- Marketing pages
- Destination pages
- Long itineraries
- Blogs
- Forms
- Future applications

without requiring additional font families.

---

## Future Multilingual Support

The selected typography should support future expansion into additional languages including, but not limited to:

- Hindi
- Tamil
- Kannada
- Telugu
- Malayalam

Where required, carefully selected companion fonts may be introduced while maintaining a visually consistent reading experience.

---

## Licensing

Preference shall be given to open-source fonts available through Google Fonts or equivalent freely licensed providers.

This ensures:

- Commercial usability
- Easy implementation
- Long-term maintainability

---

# Typography Hierarchy

The interface should maintain a consistent visual hierarchy.

Levels include:

- Display
- Hero Heading
- Page Heading
- Section Heading
- Card Heading
- Large Body
- Body
- Small Body
- Caption
- Labels

Every typography level should have a clearly defined purpose.

---

# Reading Experience Principles

Search My Vacation content should be easy to read for extended periods.

The typography system should:

- Encourage natural reading.
- Reduce cognitive effort.
- Support content scanning.
- Maintain comfortable line lengths.
- Provide generous line spacing.
- Avoid dense paragraphs.

---

# Mobile Typography

As Search My Vacation follows a Mobile-First strategy:

- Readability takes precedence over information density.
- Touch-friendly spacing should be maintained.
- Font sizes should never require zooming.
- Headlines should remain impactful without overwhelming the screen.

---

# Typography Principles

## Readability Before Decoration

Typography exists to communicate.

Decoration should never reduce readability.

---

## Consistency Builds Trust

Consistent typography creates familiarity.

Familiarity builds confidence.

---

## White Space Is Part of Typography

Spacing between letters, words, lines and paragraphs contributes as much to readability as the font itself.

---

## Emphasis Should Be Intentional

Bold text should highlight importance rather than compensate for poor layout.

---

## Every Heading Must Earn Its Place

Headings introduce ideas.

They should never be used purely for decoration.

# Official Font Selection

Following visual evaluation and alignment with the Search My Vacation brand identity, the project adopts the following typography.

## Primary Font Family

**Plus Jakarta Sans**

Fallback stack:

```
"Plus Jakarta Sans", system-ui, sans-serif
```

## Selection Rationale

Plus Jakarta Sans was selected because it provides the best balance between:

- Readability
- Warmth
- Professionalism
- Accessibility
- Modern appearance
- Premium brand perception

It aligns closely with the Search My Vacation brand personality while remaining highly legible across mobile, tablet and desktop devices.

## Future Language Support

As Search My Vacation expands into additional regional languages, compatible companion fonts may be introduced while preserving a visually consistent reading experience.

---

# Future Evolution

Typography should evolve only when improvements significantly enhance readability, accessibility or brand consistency.

Future refinements should preserve the calm, trustworthy and approachable reading experience that defines Search My Vacation.
