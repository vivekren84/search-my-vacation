# Purpose

Photography is one of the strongest trust signals for a travel brand.

The imagery used across SearchMyVacation should inspire confidence, evoke emotion, and help visitors imagine themselves already experiencing the destination.

Our photography should never feel like generic stock travel images.

Instead, every image should communicate:

> "This could be your next unforgettable experience."

---

# Brand Photography Principles

Our imagery should consistently feel:

- Authentic
- Aspirational
- Warm
- Premium
- Welcoming
- Human

Avoid imagery that feels:

- Overly edited
- Artificial
- Excessively saturated
- Empty
- Corporate
- Generic stock photography

---

# Emotional Goals

Every image should evoke one or more of the following emotions.

| Emotion | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Wonder | Discovery and exploration |
| Trust | Safe and professionally managed trips |
| Joy | Shared travel experiences |
| Freedom | Escape from routine |
| Relaxation | Stress-free holidays |
| Curiosity | Desire to explore something new |

---

# Photography Style

Preferred style:

- Natural photography
- Documentary-style moments
- Real travellers
- Genuine smiles
- Authentic experiences

Avoid:

- Posed group photos
- Fake smiling stock models
- Artificial studio lighting
- Obvious commercial stock imagery

---

# Destination Photography

Destination photos should focus on experiences rather than landmarks.

Instead of:

❌ Just showing the Eiffel Tower

Prefer:

✅ Couple walking near the Eiffel Tower at sunset

Instead of:

❌ Empty beach

Prefer:

✅ Family enjoying the beach

Instead of:

❌ Mountain landscape only

Prefer:

✅ Trekker enjoying the mountain view

People create emotional connection.

---

# Human Presence

Recommended balance:

70% Destination + People

30% Scenic Destination

The visitor should imagine themselves inside the photograph.

---

# Diversity & Inclusivity

Represent travellers across:

- Families
- Couples
- Solo travellers
- Senior travellers
- Friends
- Children
- Different ethnicities
- Different age groups

This reflects our target audience and creates inclusivity.

---

# Lighting

Preferred lighting:

- Golden Hour
- Morning sunlight
- Soft natural light
- Warm evenings

Avoid:

- Harsh noon sunlight
- Heavy flash photography
- Overexposed skies
- Dark gloomy edits

---

# Color Treatment

Photography should naturally complement the brand palette.

Images should include:

- Deep blues
- Ocean tones
- Warm sunlight
- Natural greens
- Earth textures
- Soft whites

Avoid aggressive filters.

Maintain natural color grading.

---

# Composition

Prefer:

- Rule of Thirds
- Leading Lines
- Wide cinematic framing
- Natural depth
- Open space for text overlays

Avoid:

- Cluttered backgrounds
- Busy compositions
- Cropped landmarks
- Distracting foreground objects

---

# Image Quality Standards

Minimum:

- High Resolution
- Sharp focus
- Noise-free
- Professionally edited

Landscape images should allow responsive cropping for:

Desktop

Tablet

Mobile

without losing important subjects.

---

# Homepage Hero Images

Hero imagery should immediately answer:

"Where could I go next?"

Ideal hero images include:

- Mountains
- Beaches
- Luxury stays
- Cultural streets
- Adventure activities
- Couples travelling
- Families enjoying vacations

Hero images should leave sufficient negative space for headlines and CTA buttons.

---

# Destination Cards

Destination thumbnails should:

- Clearly represent the location
- Have consistent color grading
- Similar framing style
- Similar horizon placement
- High visual clarity

Avoid mixing illustration with photography.

---

# AI-Generated Images

AI-generated visuals may be used when:

- No authentic photography exists
- Creating conceptual travel inspiration
- Seasonal marketing campaigns
- Future blog illustrations

AI images must:

- Look photorealistic
- Match brand color grading
- Avoid exaggerated fantasy scenes
- Avoid unrealistic human anatomy
- Maintain consistent lighting

AI images should always be reviewed by a human before publication.

---

# Image Overlays

When text appears over photography:

- Use subtle dark overlays
- Maintain WCAG AA contrast
- Never obscure faces
- Preserve destination details

---

# Accessibility

Every image must include:

- Meaningful alt text
- Descriptive captions where appropriate
- Avoid embedding important text inside images

---

# Image Sources

Preferred sources:

1. Original SearchMyVacation photography
2. Partner-provided destination imagery
3. Licensed professional photography
4. Carefully curated premium stock photography
5. Approved AI-generated imagery

---

# Summary

Our imagery should make visitors feel that every journey is:

- Personal
- Authentic
- Memorable
- Safe
- Inspiring

The goal is not merely to show destinations, but to help users imagine themselves experiencing them.
