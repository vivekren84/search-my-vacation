This document defines the official brand identity of Search My Vacation.

It establishes the personality, positioning, values, messaging and visual direction that guide every customer interaction.

The Brand Identity serves as the single source of truth for Marketing, Product Design, User Experience, Engineering and future AI-assisted content generation.

Every public-facing touchpoint should align with the principles defined in this document.

# Brand Vision

To become India's most trusted Travel Experience Platform by creating personalised journeys that travellers remember for a lifetime.

---

# Brand Mission

To design meaningful travel experiences through personalised planning, trusted advice and exceptional service.

Every traveller deserves a journey that reflects their interests, preferences and dreams—not a one-size-fits-all itinerary.

---

# Brand Positioning

Search My Vacation is a Travel Experience Company that designs handcrafted journeys around every traveller’s unique aspirations, preferences and budget.

Unlike traditional Online Travel Agencies (OTAs), Search My Vacation focuses on consultation, customisation and long-term customer relationships rather than transactional bookings.

---

# Brand Promise

Every journey begins with understanding the traveller.

Every itinerary is handcrafted around the guest's requirements—not selected from a predefined package.

# Brand Terminology

The language used throughout Search My Vacation should consistently reinforce the company's positioning as a Travel Experience Company.

Customer-facing communication should use terminology that reflects experiences, relationships and personalised journeys rather than transactional travel sales.

| Avoid (Customer-Facing) | Preferred |
|--------------------------|-----------|
| Package | Journey |
| Tour Package | Curated Journey |
| Holiday Package | Holiday Experience |
| Travel Package | Personalised Journey |
| Booking | Travel Planning |
| Customer | Traveller / Guest |
| Travel Agent | Travel Expert / Travel Consultant |
| Discount (as primary messaging) | Travel Benefit (where appropriate) |
| Coupon | Experience Benefit / Travel Credit (where appropriate) |

### Usage Guidelines

- **Destination** refers to *where* a traveller can go.
- **Experience** refers to *how* a traveller wants to travel.
- **Journey** refers to the personalised itinerary designed for an individual traveller.

Operational or technical documentation may continue using traditional industry terminology where appropriate, but all customer-facing communication should follow the preferred terminology defined above.
