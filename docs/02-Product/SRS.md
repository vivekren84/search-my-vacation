## Homepage Module

The Homepage is the digital front door of Search My Vacation. It should
build trust, inspire exploration and encourage visitors to begin
planning.

Experience Goal: Visitors should feel welcome, inspired, confident and
curious.

Business Objective: Build trust, encourage exploration and generate
enquiries.

Related Features: M1-001 through M1-007.

Example Requirement: FR-HOME-001 Hero Section shall include logo,
navigation, heading, supporting heading, primary CTA and optimized hero
imagery with responsive behaviour.

Product Owner Note: The Homepage should never become cluttered. Every
section must build trust or move the traveller closer to planning.
