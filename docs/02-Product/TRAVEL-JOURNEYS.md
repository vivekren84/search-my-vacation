# Purpose

This document defines how travellers interact with Search My Vacation during Milestone 1 of Project Compass.

Rather than describing website pages, this document describes customer experiences.

The journeys defined here become the foundation for the Software Requirements Specification (SRS), User Experience (UX), Information Architecture and future development.

---

# Scope

This document covers **Milestone 1 – Website Replacement** only.

Future milestones such as Login, Wishlist, CRM, AI Assistant, Customer Dashboard and Group Tours are intentionally excluded.

---

# Journey Overview

Every traveller should progress naturally through the following journey.

```
Discover Search My Vacation
        ↓
Explore Travel Collections
        ↓
Build Travel Confidence
        ↓
Plan My Trip
        ↓
Begin Relationship
```

---

# Journey 1 – Discover Search My Vacation

## Goal

Help first-time visitors immediately understand who Search My Vacation is and why they should trust us.

---

## Typical Entry Points

- Google Search
- Instagram
- Facebook
- WhatsApp
- Referral
- Direct Website Visit

---

## Customer Questions

- Can I trust this company?
- What makes them different?
- Do they customise trips?
- Are they suitable for my family?

---

## Website Experience

The homepage should communicate:

- More Than a Trip. It's an Experience.
- Travel Collections
- Featured Destinations
- Why Choose Search My Vacation
- Customer Testimonials
- Call-to-Action

---

## Pain Points Today

- Generic travel websites look similar.
- Too much information too early.
- Customers struggle to identify trustworthy travel planners.

---

## Search My Vacation Opportunity

Build trust before asking customers to enquire.

---

## Features Required

- Homepage
- Hero Banner
- Testimonials
- Why Choose Us
- Travel Collections
- Featured Destinations

---

## Moment of Delight

"This already feels different from other travel websites."

---

## Success Criteria

Visitor continues exploring the website.

---

# Journey 2 – Explore Travel Collections

## Goal

Help customers discover the right type of holiday instead of forcing them to choose a destination immediately.

---

## Customer Questions

- I want a holiday, but where should I go?
- Which destination suits my family?
- What are my options?

---

## Website Experience

Customers browse collections such as:

- Weekend Getaways
- Family Holidays
- Romantic Escapes
- Adventure Tours
- International Holidays
- Senior Friendly
- Nature Escapes

Selecting a collection displays curated destinations.

---

## Pain Points Today

Customers often know they want a vacation but don't know the destination.

---

## Search My Vacation Opportunity

Guide customers through inspiration instead of overwhelming them with destinations.

---

## Features Required

- Travel Collections
- Destination Cards
- Filters
- Collection Pages

---

## Moment of Delight

"I didn't realise there were so many options that actually suit my needs."

---

## Success Criteria

Customer opens a destination page.

---

# Journey 3 – Build Travel Confidence

## Goal

Educate customers before asking them to make an enquiry.

---

## Customer Questions

- Is this destination suitable for us?
- What is the best time to visit?
- How many days should I plan?
- Is this worth my budget?

---

## Website Experience

Each destination should include:

- Destination Overview
- Highlights
- Best Time to Visit
- Sample Itinerary
- FAQs
- Travel Tips
- Suggested Duration

(Travel Confidence Score will be introduced in Milestone 3.)

---

## Pain Points Today

Customers spend hours researching across multiple websites.

---

## Search My Vacation Opportunity

Become the trusted source of travel guidance.

---

## Features Required

- Destination Detail Page
- Itinerary Viewer
- FAQ Section
- Travel Tips

---

## Moment of Delight

"I already trust these people before even speaking with them."

---

## Success Criteria

Customer clicks **Plan My Trip**.

---

# Journey 4 – Plan My Trip

## Goal

Convert interested visitors into qualified enquiries.

---

## Customer Questions

- Can this itinerary be customised?
- What will it cost?
- Can I speak to someone?

---

## Website Experience

Instead of a traditional form, customers experience a conversational enquiry flow.

Example:

- Where would you like to travel?
- When are you planning to travel?
- Number of travellers?
- Approximate budget?
- Any special requirements?

---

## Pain Points Today

Static enquiry forms feel impersonal and discouraging.

---

## Search My Vacation Opportunity

Create the feeling of chatting with a travel consultant.

---

## Features Required

- Interactive Enquiry Flow
- WhatsApp Integration
- Contact Form
- Lead Capture

---

## Moment of Delight

"This feels much more personal than filling out a form."

---

## Success Criteria

Enquiry successfully submitted.

---

# Journey 5 – Begin the Relationship

## Goal

Reassure customers that their enquiry is being personally handled.

---

## Customer Expectations

- Confirmation received
- Clear next steps
- Expected response time
- Confidence that someone will contact them

---

## Website Experience

Immediately after submission:

- Thank You message
- Confirmation Email
- WhatsApp acknowledgement (future)
- Travel consultant follow-up

---

## Search My Vacation Opportunity

Transform an enquiry into the beginning of a long-term relationship.

---

## Features Required

- Thank You Page
- Email Confirmation
- Contact Information

---

## Moment of Delight

"I already feel taken care of."

---

## Success Criteria

Customer waits confidently for follow-up.
