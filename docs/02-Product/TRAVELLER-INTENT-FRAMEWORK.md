# Traveller Intent Framework

**Version:** 1.0 (Draft)

**Status:** Foundation Document

**Owner:** Team Satvi

---

# Purpose

Search My Vacation believes travellers begin their journey with an intention, emotion or life moment—not a destination.

This framework establishes how Search My Vacation understands a traveller before recommending destinations, experiences and itineraries.

The framework serves as the foundation for the homepage, future AI experiences, recommendation engines and traveller personalisation.

---

# Guiding Philosophy

> People don't search for destinations.
>
> They search for the life they want to experience for a few precious days.

---

# Design Principles

Every traveller interaction should:

- Begin with understanding rather than selling.
- Inspire before recommending.
- Curate rather than overwhelm.
- Feel conversational rather than transactional.
- Focus on emotions before logistics.

# Core Framework

## Stage 1 — Travel Intent

Understand why the traveller wants to travel.

The wording of these intents will be refined through dedicated UX workshops and user validation.

Current draft intent categories include:

- Slow Down
- Time Together
- Family Time
- Adventure
- Discovery
- Celebration
- Nature
- Quick Escape
- Luxury
- Surprise Me

---

## Stage 2 — Traveller Context

Understand who the traveller is travelling with.

Examples include:

- Solo
- Partner
- Family
- Friends
- Parents
- Multi-generation Family
- Colleagues
- Group

---

## Stage 3 — Planning Preferences

Capture practical planning details.

Examples:

- Budget
- Duration
- Month
- Domestic / International
- Visa Readiness
- Food Preferences
- Travel Pace
- Accessibility Requirements

---

## Stage 4 — Curated Experiences

Search My Vacation recommends curated journeys inspired by the traveller's intent and preferences rather than presenting a generic catalogue of destinations.

---

# Signature Experience

## Surprise Me

"Surprise Me" represents Search My Vacation's philosophy of helping travellers discover journeys they may never have considered.

For Release 1, this experience will be manually curated.

Future releases may introduce personalised and AI-assisted discovery.

---

# Success Measures

The framework is considered successful when visitors:

- Continue scrolling after the Hero section.
- Engage with traveller intents.
- Explore curated journeys.
- Discover destinations beyond their initial expectations.
- Feel understood before reaching the enquiry stage.

---

# Future Improvements

The following areas require dedicated workshops before Version 2.0:

- Intent wording and emotional language.
- Brand voice guidelines for traveller interactions.
- Intent hierarchy and prioritisation.
- Mapping intents to destinations.
- AI-assisted traveller discovery.

---

> "More Than a Trip. It's an Experience."

This framework exists to ensure every interaction across Search My Vacation consistently delivers on that promise.
