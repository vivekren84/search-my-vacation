# Purpose

This roadmap defines the phased evolution of Search My Vacation.

Rather than attempting to build the complete vision at once, the platform will evolve through carefully planned milestones that deliver measurable business value while maintaining a scalable technical foundation.

Every milestone should produce something valuable that can be released to customers.

# Product Vision

Our long-term vision is to build India's most trusted Travel Relationship Platform.

The roadmap below describes how we will progressively achieve that vision.

---

# Guiding Principles

Every milestone must satisfy the following principles:

- Deliver measurable business value.
- Improve customer trust.
- Keep the platform scalable.
- Build only what is needed now.
- Design today for tomorrow's expansion.

---

# Milestone 1 – Website Replacement

## Objective

Replace the existing WordPress website with a modern, responsive platform that reflects the quality of Search My Vacation and provides a significantly better customer experience.

## Business Goal

Launch a professional website within approximately four weeks without disrupting existing business operations.

## Features

### Homepage

- Modern landing page
- Hero section
- Search My Vacation story
- Travel Collections
- Featured destinations
- Why Choose Us
- Testimonials
- Call to Action

---

### Destinations

- Domestic
- International
- Destination details
- Sample itineraries
- Image gallery

---

### Travel Collections

Examples:

- Weekend Getaways
- Family Holidays
- Romantic Escapes
- Senior Friendly
- Adventure
- International

---

### About Us

Completely redesigned.

Focus on trust rather than company history.

---

### Testimonials

Integrated throughout the website instead of a standalone page.

---

### Contact

- Contact form
- WhatsApp
- Email
- Google Maps

---

### Interactive Enquiry

Replace the traditional enquiry form with a conversational experience.

Customers should feel like they are chatting with a travel consultant.

---

### Blog

Basic blog capability.

---

### Legal

- Privacy Policy
- Terms & Conditions

---

### Technical

- Responsive
- SEO Friendly
- Fast Loading
- Secure
- Hosted on Vercel

---

## Success Criteria

Business

- Existing website successfully replaced.
- Existing enquiry capability maintained.
- Customers perceive the new website as significantly more professional.
- Positive feedback received from existing customers.

Technical

- Mobile responsive.
- Lighthouse score above target (to be defined later).
- No critical production issues.

# Release Strategy

The platform will follow iterative releases.

Major Milestones deliver business capabilities.

Minor Releases improve existing capabilities.

Patch Releases focus on quality improvements and bug fixes.

---

# Definition of Done

A milestone is considered complete only when:

- Business objectives are achieved.
- Technical quality standards are met.
- Documentation is updated.
- Source code is committed.
- Production deployment is successful.
- Stakeholder review is completed.
